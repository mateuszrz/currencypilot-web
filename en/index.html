Calculator

Does the maths as you type

Type 55 + 35 and see the amount in the other currency straight away. The result updates on every keystroke — no switching to a separate calculator and back.

You can enter the amount on either side. Tap the bottom field and the app works out how much you need in the source currency.

Freshness

You always know when the rate is from

The European Central Bank publishes euro reference rates on business days at around 16:00 CET. On weekends and holidays the app shows the last published rates and marks their date clearly. Without a connection it works on data stored on your device — and says plainly that it is older than today.

Rates are for reference. The full description of the data source is on the “About” screen and in the privacy policy.
